# Design Goals & Mechanics

This developer-facing page explains why Veiled Dominion exists and the mechanical choices behind it.

Goals
- Encourage strategic restraint: reward players for preventing escalation.
- Four-player dynamics: foster temporary alliances and shifting threats.
- Tactical clarity: rules designed to minimize downtime and ambiguity.

- Restraint scoring: how and why we quantify restraint instead of just captures.
- The Veil mechanic: design motivation — reduces information, increases bluffing.
